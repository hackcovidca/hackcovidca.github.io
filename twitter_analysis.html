Twitter Analysis

Word cloud analysis of users' response on Twitter

LA County Public Health Twitter Response

Updated on 02/03/2021

We did a toy project analyzing 29 responses to two Twitter announcement related to vaccine registration. We use Python to tokenize the words and draw word cloud from thoses 29 responses. From the word cloud, we can tell that the word "appointment" has been mentioned the most. Although the dataset is quite small and limited, it gives a glimse of snapshot look about people's concerns at that very moment.
